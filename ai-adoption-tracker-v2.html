AI Adoption Tracker

A running log of real-world cases where AI adoption decisions were made under urgency, competitive pressure, or social proof — and what followed. Connected to the research behind The Crisis Adoption Problem and its companion essays.

◈ How this works: Each search uses Claude with live web access to surface current cases, then structures them against the adoption framework from my dissertation research. Cases are scored for trigger type, evaluation depth, and documented outcome. This is a research aid, not a judgment — the goal is pattern recognition, not verdict.
